# 🌱 AgriFlow AI

AgriFlow AI is an intelligent agricultural supply chain management platform designed to help farmers and distributors reduce food waste through AI-powered risk prediction, route optimization, and sustainability analytics.

## 🚀 Features

* Harvest Management
* Shipment Management
* AI-Powered Risk Analysis
* Spoilage Risk Prediction
* Sustainability Scoring
* Carbon Emission Tracking
* Route Optimization
* Route Visualization Map
* AI Recommendations
* Dashboard Analytics
* Priority Actions Monitoring

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

* Laravel 12
* PHP 8.3
* MySQL
* Tailwind CSS
* Chart.js
* Leaflet.js
* OpenRouter AI
* OpenRouteService API

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Food waste remains one of the major challenges in agricultural supply chains. Delays in distribution, inefficient routes, and limited decision support often result in spoilage and financial losses.

## 💡 Solution

AgriFlow AI helps stakeholders make smarter logistics decisions by combining artificial intelligence, route optimization, and sustainability-focused analytics into a single platform.

## 📊 Key Capabilities

* Predict spoilage risk before products expire
* Analyze shipment sustainability
* Monitor carbon emissions
* Visualize delivery routes
* Generate AI-based recommendations
* Track agricultural distribution performance
